Cancellation & Refund Policy
A) Cancellations can be requested online via your Rabbitstay account or by emailing our
support team.
B) Refunds will be processed only after Rabbitstay receives confirmation from the
concerned hotel or service provider.
C) Refunds will be credited to your original payment method within 72 hours of Rabbitstay
receiving the refund from the hotel or provider.
D) If you do not show up (no-show) or do not utilize the booking, No refunds are offered.
E) Some hotel bookings may be non-refundable or subject to additional charges, as
specified at the time of booking and by the hotel’s specific terms.
F) For special offers like “Zero Cancellation” (if applicable), you may be eligible for a full
refund if you cancel 24 hours prior to check-in, provided you paid for cancellation
protection at the time of booking. This does not cover the protection fee itself, and
additional terms may apply
G) If Rabbitstay finds a booking is based on inaccurate or fraudulent information, we may
cancel the booking without refund.
These policies provide a comprehensive, industry-standard framework customized for a hotel
booking platform.
