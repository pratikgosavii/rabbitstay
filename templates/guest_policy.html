Guest Policy
A) All guests must provide a valid government-issued photo ID at check-in, as required by
the hotel.
B) The number of guests in the booking must not exceed the room’s allowed occupancy.
C) Some hotels may have additional restrictions (e.g., non-local guest bans, minimum age
for check-in, restrictions on unmarried couples, etc.). Check each hotel’s rules before
booking.
D) Rabbitstay does not control hotels’ internal guest policies. Disputes regarding guest
eligibility or check-in requirements are to be resolved with the hotel directly.
E) Rabbitstay may refuse bookings deemed to violate local laws or the policies of partner
hotels.
F) Any behavior that compromises public safety, causes health hazards, or violates
government orders may result in denied accommodation at the discretion of the hotel or
Rabbitstay.
These policies provide a comprehensive, industry-standard framework customized for a hotel
booking platform.
